## Best Practice Tips for WhatsApp Ads:
1. **Use emojis sparingly** - Don't overdo it
2. **Keep it concise** - WhatsApp users prefer short messages
3. **Include clear CTA** - Tell users exactly what to do
4. **Use formatting** - Bold/italic for emphasis (*text*)
5. **Mobile-friendly** - Test on phone screen
6. **Include link** - Make it easy to access
7. **Create urgency** - Use action words
